FAQs

  1. How does Aura Network differ from other chains?
    For Aura Network, interoperability is our competitive advantage, allowing persistent and transferable NFTs and raising asset liquidity. We are shaping NFTs’ culture and expanding the use of NFTs across various industries to go towards a decentralized future.
  2. Why building on Cosmos?
    There are two main reasons why Aura Network chose Cosmos: agility and cosmos IBC. Cosmos is an ecosystem of blockchains that can scale and interoperate with each other. It solves the limitations of “the old blockchains”. The Inter-Blockchain Communication protocol (IBC) allows blockchains to talk to each other. The backbone of the Cosmos ecosystem, IBC handles transport across different sovereign blockchains. Building on Cosmos can help Aura Network accelerate Global NFTs adoption and become a catalyst for NFTs culture.
